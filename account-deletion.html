Steps to Request Account Deletion

  1. Compose a new email from your registered email address.
  2. Set the recipient's email address to: info@linebits.co
  3. In the subject line, type: Data Deletion Request
  4. In the body of the email, include the following information:
    • Your full name
    • Your registered email address
    • Your user ID (if applicable)
    • A brief reason for the account deletion request
  5. Double-check all the information for accuracy.
  6. You will receive a confirmation email once your request is processed.
  7. Your account and associated data will be deleted within 7-14 business days.
